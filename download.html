What the desktop app adds

Silent printing

Receipts and labels print straight to your thermal printer with no browser print dialog in the way.

Kiosk mode

Full-screen, distraction-free POS view. No address bar, no tabs, no accidental navigation.

System tray

Minimize to the tray and the app keeps running. Click to bring it back instantly.

Start on boot

Optional auto-launch when Windows starts. Register is ready before you open the doors.

Installation

1
Click the download button above

Saves RetroVault-Setup.exe to your Downloads folder.

2
Double-click the installer

Windows may show a SmartScreen prompt — see note below.

3
Follow the install wizard

Takes about 30 seconds. The app launches automatically when finished.

4
Sign in with your RetroVault account

Use the same email and password you got in your welcome email.

About the Windows SmartScreen warning

When you run the installer, Windows may show a blue "Windows protected your PC" prompt. This happens because RetroVault is a small developer and the installer isn't yet signed with an EV code-signing certificate. To proceed, click More info, then Run anyway. The installer is safe.

System requirements

  • OS: Windows 10 (64-bit) or later
  • Memory: 4 GB RAM minimum, 8 GB recommended
  • Storage: 300 MB free disk space
  • Internet: Required (RetroVault is cloud-based)
  • Printers: Any Windows-installed printer works for receipts and labels
