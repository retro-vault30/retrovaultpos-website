Features
Built for the way your store actually works
POS

Full Point of Sale

Sell, return, and exchange. Accept cash, card, store credit, or split payments. Attach customers, apply discounts, and print receipts.

Trade-Ins

Trade-In Calculator

Look up any game by title, get a cash and credit offer based on live market pricing and your configured margin rules.

Inventory

Inventory Management

Track every item individually with SKUs, condition grading, cost, price, and cover art. Import inventory in bulk via CSV.

Consignment

Consignment Tracking

Set split percentages per consignor, track sold items, calculate payouts, and give consignors a self-service portal to check their balance.

Gift Cards

Gift Cards

Issue and redeem gift cards at checkout. Supports both generated codes and pre-printed physical cards with custom barcodes.

Loyalty

Loyalty Program

Points-based loyalty with customer profiles, purchase history, store credit, and configurable birthday perks.

Stock Counts

Stock Counting

Run blind or guided stock counts by platform or category. Review variances, drill down to individual SKUs, and mark missing items as lost.

Labels

SKU Label Printing

Print price tags and shelf labels with barcodes directly from inventory. Compatible with standard thermal label printers.

Reports

Reporting & P&L

Daily, weekly, and monthly sales reports. Profit and loss by category, end-of-day summaries, and transaction history.
